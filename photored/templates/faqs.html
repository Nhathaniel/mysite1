Photo Website FAQs

  • How do I create an account?
    To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button and follow the instructions to register.
  • How do I reset my password?
    You can reset your password by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and following the instructions.

  • How do I upload photos?
    You can upload photos by clicking on the "Upload" button and selecting the images you want to upload from your device.
  • What file formats are supported?
    We support JPEG, PNG, and GIF file formats for photo uploads.
